  Membership Benefits

Members of TAR Hotpot could enjoy online appointments; your spending at TAR Hotpot will accumulate Star points which can be used to redeem cash vouchers at all TAR Hotpot branches.

  Membership Registration Method

You may submit your registration information and become a member of TAR Hotpot after approval at the order pad offered at all TAR Hotpot branches.

Membership Registration Fee

RM20 (One Year Validity)

  Membership Star Points

Present TAR Hotpot membership card upon checkout at our restaurant and you are eligible to get 1 Star point for every RM10 spent.

Star Points Usage

50 Star points = RM 20 voucher

100 Star points = RM 50 voucher
