Our food is the best.

  The TAR HOTPOT Restaurant is a buffet-style hot pot restaurant that serves a variety of food from around the world. Our restaurant was established in the year 2018 at Jalan Genting Kelang, Setapak. In three years, it has quickly branched out three more different outlets across three states, located in Johor, Selangor and Penang. Up to the year 2021, we have more than 40 staff working in our outlets serving our beloved customers.
