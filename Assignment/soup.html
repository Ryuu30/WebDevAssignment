Soup Bases

Thai Tom Yum

If you love hot and sour, then this is for you!

A simple, but flavorful, Thai tom yum soup made with fresh lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, fish sauce, chiles, and shrimp.

Tomato

Not a fan of spicy but want something sour? we've got you covered!

Blanched and cut tomatoes into pieces and fried with aromatic garlic onions. To make the sauce thicken and sourer, we added ketchup and fry until thickened to make our tomato soup base.

Herbal Chicken

Want something lighter? Try this!

The soup base is double-boiled with seven Chinese herbs, red dates, goji berries, angelica root, astragalus root, codonopsis root, Sichuan lovage rhizome and American ginseng. Healthy and flavourful.

Mala Spicy

Up for something extra spicy? You have to try this!

Mala is a spicy and numbing seasoning made from Sichuan peppercorn and chili pepper. It has rich tastes with red color and becomes more fragrant while boiling. Try and overcome the numbing sensation, if you can.

Laksa

Let's not forget our classic spicy Malaysian soup!

Made with a blend of lemongrass, dried chilies, shallots, galanga, turmeric, spices, dried shrimp and shrimp paste, all blended up in the food processor, then saut√©ed in a little oil to deepen the flavor. Mix in coconut milk and our classic laksa soup is done. Ready to warm you up.
