FAQ

For any other questions or concerns please contact us.

What are the prices?

All our prices are fixed. Excluding news from our official website or restaurants, please do not believe any news about promotions from outsiders.

Kids (under 12 years old): RM 39.90
Adult : RM 49.90
Senior citizen (60 years old and above): RM 39.90

How can I cancel my reservation?

To help our restaurants manage their seatings, please inform the selected branch at least an hour before the time of your reservation.

How can I be certain my booking has been confirmed?

On completion of your booking, you will receive either a confirmation email or message.If you did not receive a confirmation email or message within 24 hours of making your booking, please contact us directly to ensure that your booking has been confirmed.

What happens if I'm running late?

Please contact the respective branch to cancel or rebook your reservation, or else your reservation will be cancelled after 30 minutes of the scheduled time.

Why do you need my email address and phone number?

We will use the email and phone number provided to contact you if there are any changes to your reservation or as a reminder of the reservation if necessary.
